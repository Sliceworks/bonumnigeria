About Bonum Nigeria Limited

Incorporated in 2007, Bonum Nigeria Limited operates as a multidisciplinary petroleum engineering and consultancy firm serving Nigeria's oil and gas industry with offices across key operational regions including Lagos, Port Harcourt, Eket, Bonny, and Enugu.

Company Overview

Bonum Nigeria Limited (RC-722502) is a private limited liability company specializing in petroleum engineering services, procurement, construction, project management, and technical consulting for the Nigerian oil and gas industry.

Our firm provides integrated energy solutions across the petroleum value chain, from upstream production facilities through downstream refinery operations, with particular expertise in process engineering, refinery maintenance, and large-scale infrastructure project management for international oil companies and government agencies.

Operating from strategic locations in Lagos, Eket, Bonny, Enugu, and Port Harcourt, we maintain proximity to major oil and gas installations while ensuring rapid deployment of technical personnel and resources throughout Nigeria's petroleum sector.
